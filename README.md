Daily Questions
A lightweight daily journal app for morning and evening reflection — no login, no tracking, no data stored anywhere.

Try it
**[Open the app →](https://joeredd.github.io/eight-questions/)**

The questions
Every morning and every evening, work through three sets of questions:
Show-Up Questions — to ground yourself in gratitude, love, and joy
• What am I grateful for?
• Who do I love?
• Why am I so happy?

Step-Up Questions — to stay accountable to what matters
• What am I committed to doing that I may have been neglecting?
• How committed am I to doing it?

Give-Back Questions — to set intention, clarify your wish, and reconnect with purpose
• What is my intention today? (morning) / What is my intention tomorrow? (evening)
• What is my wish?
• Why am I here?

Features
• Morning and evening sessions, each with their own answers
• Privacy controls — mark individual answers private before sharing
• Copy your session to share with others
• Printable version included (daily-questions-print.html)
